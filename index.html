About Me

Hi, my name is Fatima Fakih. This is a photo of my team and I after we'd just won the FFA Champion Of Champions Cup. one of my fondest memories. Best.Time.Ever. Most recently I find myself endeavouring on a journey to explore something new to unlock more of myself. Hence joining this Coding Bootcamp. I really look forward to all the new learning and making everlasting connections with people along the way.
